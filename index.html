INVENTORY

HK416

The Heckler & Koch HK416 stands as a pinnacle of modern firearms engineering. Designed as an improvement upon the conventional AR-15 platform, the HK416 boasts exceptional reliability, accuracy, and versatility. This assault rifle utilizes a short-stroke gas piston system, which reduces fouling and heat buildup, resulting in increased durability and consistent performance even in adverse conditions. Its ergonomic design incorporates ambidextrous controls, allowing for ease of use by both left- and right-handed shooters.

FN F2X

The FN F2X is a gas-operated, fully automatic and ambidextrous bullpup rifle. Both the safety system and trigger mechanism were adopted from the P90 personal defense weapon; the selector toggle is a rotating disc located below the trigger. The fire selector doubles as the weapon's manual safety and secures the firearm against accidental discharge (the selector/safety disc has 3 settings: "S" safe, "1" semi-automatic mode, "A" fully automatic fire). The "safe" setting disables the trigger. The hammers, group pins, and springs are steel while all other components are nylon injection molding. The shell of the rifle is made of composite materials.
